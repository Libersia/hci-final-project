Resident Evil 7: Biohazard

Resident Evil 7: Biohazard is a survival horror game that takes place in Dulvey, Louisiana. The game follows Ethan Winters as he searches for his missing wife, Mia, who has mysteriously disappeared.

Ethan's investigation leads him to the Baker family estate, a derelict plantation house where the family has been infected by a mutagenic organism that turns them into violent, psychotic hosts.

The game introduces a more grounded horror experience, combining exploration, puzzle-solving, and combat while maintaining the unsettling atmosphere of the Baker household. Modernized graphics and immersive audio enhance the fear factor.
