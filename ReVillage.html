TOP SECRET
Resident Evil Village

Resident Evil Village continues the story of Ethan Winters, following the events of Resident Evil 7: Biohazard. Ethan is drawn to a mysterious Eastern European village while searching for his kidnapped daughter.

The village is populated by hostile mutated villagers and terrifying creatures under the control of the enigmatic Mother Miranda. Survival requires exploration, combat, and resource management while uncovering the dark secrets of the village and its rulers.

This installment expands on the survival horror formula with modern graphics, first-person gameplay, and a mix of gothic horror and action elements, delivering one of the most intense experiences in the series to date.
