Resident Evil 2

Resident Evil 2 follows rookie police officer Leon S. Kennedy and college student Claire Redfield as they attempt to survive the outbreak of the T-virus in Raccoon City.

The city has been overrun by the infected, including zombie civilians and mutated creatures. Both protagonists must navigate the urban environment, solve puzzles, and escape from relentless enemies while uncovering the secrets behind the Umbrella Corporation’s bioweapon experiments.

This remake modernizes the original 1998 release with updated graphics, reimagined environments, and improved gameplay mechanics, preserving the tension and horror that defined the classic survival horror experience.
