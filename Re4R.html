Resident Evil 4 Remake

Resident Evil 4 Remake is a survival horror action game that follows U.S. government agent Leon S. Kennedy on a high-risk rescue mission. Six years after the Raccoon City biological disaster, Leon is assigned to locate and rescue the kidnapped daughter of the President of the United States.

His investigation leads him to a mysterious rural European village where residents have been infected by a parasitic organism known as Las Plagas. The outbreak causes villagers to behave with extreme hostility and intelligence, making survival far more dangerous than previous viral outbreaks.

The remake modernizes the original game by improving graphics, gameplay mechanics, enemy AI, and environmental storytelling while keeping the intense horror atmosphere that defined the original release.
